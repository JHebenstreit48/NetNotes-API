# What Is Internet Telephony?

<hr class="dividerSection" />

Making telephone calls over the internet has become increasingly common. An internet telephony client uses peer-to-peer technology similar to instant messaging.

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Internet telephony makes use of <span class="emphasis">Voice over IP (VoIP)</span> technology.</li>
    <li>VoIP converts analog voice signals into digital data.</li>
    <li>That digital voice data is encapsulated into IP packets, which carry the call through the network.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<hr class="dividerSection" />

## Setting Up an Internet Telephony Client

<hr class="dividerSection" />

<div class="centeredNumberedList">

1. <span class="emphasis">Choosing a Username</span>

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Once the client software is installed, the user selects a unique name so that calls can be received from other users.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

2. <span class="emphasis">Audio Setup</span>

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Speakers and a microphone are required, either built-in or separate.</li>
    <li>A headset is frequently plugged into the computer to serve as a phone.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

</div>

<hr class="dividerSection" />

## Making a Call

<hr class="dividerSection" />

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Calls to other users of the same service are made by selecting their username from a list.</li>
    <li>A call to a regular telephone, either a landline or a cell phone, requires a gateway to access the <span class="emphasis">Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN)</span>.</li>
    <li>Depending on the service, calls to the PSTN may have associated charges.</li>
    <li>The specific protocols and destination ports used by an internet telephony application can vary depending on the software.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<hr class="dividerSection" />

## VoIP Beyond Standalone Phone Clients

<hr class="dividerSection" />

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Early internet telephony was often a standalone, single-purpose client, its whole job was placing calls.</li>
    <li>Voice and video calling in modern general-purpose messaging apps, such as Discord, Microsoft Teams, and WhatsApp, is also built on VoIP technology.</li>
    <li>In these apps, VoIP is one feature among many, rather than the entire product.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
