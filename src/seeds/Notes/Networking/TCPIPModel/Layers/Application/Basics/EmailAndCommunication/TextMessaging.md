# What Is Text Messaging?

<hr class="dividerSection" />

Text messaging is one of the most popular communication tools in use today. Text messaging software is built into many online applications, smartphone apps, and social media sites.

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Text messages may also be called instant messages, direct messages, private messages, or chat messages.</li>
    <li>Text messaging enables users to communicate in real-time over the internet.</li>
    <li>On a computer, text messaging is usually accessed through a web-based client integrated into a social media or information sharing site, and these clients usually only connect to other users of the same site.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<hr class="dividerSection" />

## Standalone Text Messaging Clients

<hr class="dividerSection" />

A number of standalone text message clients also exist, available across a wide variety of operating systems and devices, typically including a mobile version.

<div class="centeredBullet">
  <ul class="diamondBullets fullWidthBullet">
    <li>Examples include Cisco Webex Teams, Microsoft Teams, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Slack, and Discord, among many others.</li>
    <li>In addition to text messages, these clients often support the transfer of documents, video, music, and audio files.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
