# Straight-Through vs Crossover

---

## Straight-Through Cables

---

A straight-through cable uses the **same wiring standard on both ends**.

Examples:

- T568A → T568A
- T568B → T568B

Used for:

- PC → Switch
- Router → Switch
- PC → Router

---

## Crossover Cables

---

A crossover cable uses **different wiring standards on each end**.

Example:

- T568A → T568B

This swaps the transmit and receive pairs.

Used for:

- PC → PC
- Switch → Switch (older equipment)
- Router → Router

Modern devices often support **Auto-MDI/MDI-X**, so crossover cables are rarely required today.
